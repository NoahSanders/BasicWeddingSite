About Noah & Parker

Noah and Parker met in 2013, both invited by mutual friends on a canoe trip of destiny. As fate would have it, Parker’s canoe-buddy proved to be garbage at canoeing. Noah switched boats to avoid catastrophe and helped Parker navigate the treacherous rapids of the roaring Harpeth River. Along the way, the two hit it off.

Noah however, had made a grave mistake; he forgot to invite his insanely intelligent and attractive best friend forever, Christina Gaines. Without her there, Noah had no one to wing-woman for him and help get the show on the road. It took Noah two weeks to finally ask for Parker’s phone number. Thankfully, Parker has the patience of a sweet baby angel and knew that her womanly wiles would ultimately work their magic. In the end she was right, as is usually the case.

Now an elite power couple with the desire for total world domination, Parker and Noah are ready for the next step in their relationship: marriage. They ask their family and friends to join them on January 20th to celebrate their love and the joining of their two families as one superior family destined to lead the future of mankind.

I am Christina Gaines and I approve this message.
